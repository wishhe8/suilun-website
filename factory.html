5 Core Workshops

End-to-End Production
Under One Roof

Every process — raw material to packaged finished product — happens in-house. No outsourcing. No compromise on quality.

01
Workshop 01

Pressing & Stamping

High-speed steel cutting and cold-rolling presses form raw material into precise hinge leaves, brackets, and components. Automated stamping lines run at tolerances measured in fractions of a millimeter.

  • High-tonnage progressive stamping presses
  • Cold-roll forming for bracket and slide profiles
  • Automated coil feeding and part ejection
  • In-line dimensional inspection
02
Polishing
02
Workshop 02

Polishing Workshop

Surface preparation is critical to both appearance and plating adhesion. Our polishing workshop uses automated barrel finishing and belt sanding to achieve consistent, commercial-grade surface quality across millions of parts per month.

  • Automated barrel and vibratory finishing
  • Belt and wheel polishing for premium finishes
  • Surface roughness measurement (Ra testing)
  • Pre-treatment cleaning for optimal plating adhesion
03
Workshop 03

Electroplating & Surface Treatment

Eight automated surface treatment lines deliver electroplating, electrophoresis (e-coating), and powder coating. Salt spray testing up to 1,000 hours ensures every product meets the most demanding US market corrosion standards.

  • 8 automated electroplating & e-coat lines
  • Salt spray testing up to 1,000 hours
  • Zinc, nickel, chrome, and brass plating
  • Powder coating: 50+ stock colors + custom RAL
  • Closed-loop environmental waste treatment
04
Assembly & QC
04
Workshop 04

Assembly & Quality Control

Finished components come together on high-efficiency assembly lines — pins are pressed, springs tensioned, ball bearings installed, and every unit is functionally tested before it leaves the line. UL compliance is verified on every applicable product.

  • Semi-automated pin press and riveting systems
  • Spring tensioning and calibration stations
  • Ball-bearing installation and torque testing
  • 100% functional test on spring-hinge products
  • UL compliance verification for listed products
05
Workshop 05

Packaging & Warehouse

Modern warehouse with forklift-assisted racking and ERP-integrated inventory management. Products are packaged to Home Depot, Builders FirstSource, and private-label specifications — retail-ready, direct to shelf.

  • Retail-ready blister, clamshell, and box formats
  • Private label and custom packaging programs
  • ERP-integrated inventory and order management
  • Container-load optimization for export
  • 30 km direct route to Laem Chabang Port
